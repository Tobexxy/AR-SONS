Our Services As a small, dedicated team, we offer a comprehensive range of bathroom fitting services to bring your vision for the perfect bathroom to life. We handle every aspect of the installation process with care and expertise, ensuring a smooth and stress-free experience for you.

From initial concept and planning to the final tile, we manage your entire bathroom transformation. We work with you to create a layout and choose fixtures and finishes that suit your style, needs, and budget.

Looking to refresh an outdated bathroom without a complete overhaul? We can expertly replace old suites, update tiling, improve plumbing, and give your existing bathroom a new lease of life.

Whether you desire a sleek, modern shower room or a fully accessible wet room, we have the skills and experience to create a functional and beautiful space.

Our qualified team covers all necessary plumbing alterations, ensuring everything is installed safely and to current regulations.

We provide professional tiling for walls and floors, as well as fitting other types of bathroom flooring, with meticulous attention to detail for a flawless finish.

From toilets, basins, and baths to showers, screens, radiators, and all the finishing touches, we expertly install all your chosen bathroom elements.
